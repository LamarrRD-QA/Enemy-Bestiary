Welcome to this website. This is a fan page dedicated to the enemies of the Mario & Luigi series and their random properties. Due to the recent downfall of AlphaDream (the developers behind the series), I have decided to compile a list of all the enemies that you would see adventuring through the many lovely games. You will be able to see the stats associated with the enemy, any potential weaknesses and strengths that they may have, potential locations to find them at and the chances of getting items after they've been defeated. I hope you enjoy reading through the bestiary as much as I loved documenting them and maybe you might find something that amazes you.
